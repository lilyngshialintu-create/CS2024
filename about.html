Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book a table in advance?

No, you don't need to book a table in advance. We welcome walk-ins and have plenty of seating available. However, for our special events like Book Club Nights, we recommend arriving early to secure your spot.

How much is the membership fee?

Our membership fee is $25 per month, which includes access to all our events, 10% discount on all food and beverages, and exclusive early access to new book arrivals and special promotions.

Can I bring my own book to read?

Absolutely! We encourage you to bring your own books. Our café is designed to be a comfortable reading space, and you're welcome to enjoy your personal reading material along with our coffee and treats.

Can I join events without being a member?

Yes, you can participate in most of our events without being a member. However, members get priority booking and special discounts on event fees. Some exclusive events are reserved for members only.

Can I recommend books to the cafe?

We love book recommendations! You can suggest books through our website contact form, speak to our staff directly, or leave a note in our suggestion box. We regularly update our collection based on customer recommendations.
