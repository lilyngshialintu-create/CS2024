Your Reviews

A cosy hideaway with an unbeatable book selection. The staff knew exactly what I’d like and recommended a novel that kept me glued to my seat—paired perfectly with their rich, velvety latte. Quiet corners, warm lighting, and soft playlists made it my perfect afternoon escape.

Fantastic spot for study sessions and weekend browsing. The Wi-Fi is reliable and the tables are roomy enough for notes and laptops. Would love more vegan snack options, but otherwise a five-star vibe for book lovers.

This café is a gem — shelves full of surprising finds, comfy armchairs, and coffee that warms you to the core. I always leave inspired (and with a new book). Friendly service, lovely events, and a true sense of community make it my go-to place.

Lovely atmosphere and thoughtfully curated titles. The pastries were fresh and the espresso was smooth, though seating filled up quickly on weekends. Great place for casual meetups or to bury yourself in a good book for hours

Charming, welcoming, and genuinely bookish. I attended a short author talk here and loved how intimate it felt—felt like being in a friend’s living room. The staff’s passion for literature shows in every detail, from the rotating displays to the themed drink specials.

The warm glow of the café makes it instantly inviting, while the friendly baristas create a welcoming atmosphere. With shelves full of thoughtfully chosen books, it’s a place where I can relax, explore, and always leave feeling inspired—truly my new go-to spot.
